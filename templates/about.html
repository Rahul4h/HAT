About HAT

HAT (which means “Bazar” in Bengali) is a next-generation e-commerce platform built specifically for empowering small and independent online sellers across the country. At HAT, we don’t just provide a marketplace — we offer control, transparency, and the power of trust to both sellers and buyers.

🎯 Our Mission

To enable every small business owner to sell online with complete freedom — without the burden of high courier charges or the risk of fraudulent deliveries.

💡 What Makes Us Unique

  • 🧺 Personal Delivery System: Sellers can assign their own delivery personnel to complete orders — avoiding third-party courier fees and delays. This brings down costs and boosts control.
  • 🤝 Dual Confirmation System: Our innovative delivery confirmation process requires verification from both the customer and the delivery person before marking any order as “Delivered.” This ensures transparency and protects both sides from disputes and scams.
  • 🎁 "Only For You" – Intelligent Product Suggestions: HAT uses advanced Market Basket Analysis to power the "Only For You" section on the homepage. This smart algorithm suggests the best products based on each customer’s shopping behavior, creating a personalized shopping experience.

👤 Who Is HAT For?

  • Small and local business owners
  • Home-based sellers and online entrepreneurs
  • Sellers who want to manage their own delivery and logistics
  • Customers looking for authentic, reliable, and direct-from-seller products

🛒 How We Help

  • Easy-to-use product management tools
  • Seamless order tracking and history
  • Transparent communication between buyer and seller
  • Reduced operational cost for sellers
  • Smart shopping experience for customers

❤️ Our Belief

We believe that every business — no matter how small — deserves a digital presence. HAT was built to break the barriers of cost, complexity, and fraud, and to create a fair, simple, and secure online marketplace for all.
